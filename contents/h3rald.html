The H3RALD.com domain was bought in March 2004 and since than has always been my home on the Internet. Through the years, a total of 9 major versions of this web site has been released, each featuring some change in the backend.

Currently, this site is fully static (minus a few AJAX calls) and is powered by my very own static site generator, HastySite (soon to be released) and by LiteStore (for the archive search functionality).
