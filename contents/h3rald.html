The H3RALD.com domain was bought on 28 March 2004 and since then it has always been my home on the Internet. Through the years, a total of 10 major versions of this web site has been released.

Currently, this site is fully static, it has no JavaScript, and it is powered by my very own static site generator, HastySite.
